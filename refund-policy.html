Refund Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Digital Products (Prompt Packs & Templates)

All digital product purchases are processed through Gumroad. Since digital products are delivered instantly and cannot be "returned," we handle refunds on a case-by-case basis.

When We'll Refund

  • You were unable to download the product due to a technical issue on our end.
  • The product content was significantly different from what was described on the product page.
  • You were charged multiple times for the same product (duplicate purchase).

When We Cannot Refund

  • You've already downloaded and used the product.
  • You simply changed your mind after purchase.
  • The product works as described but didn't meet your subjective expectations.

AI Services (Custom Work)

Custom AI services are non-refundable once deliverables have been provided. If you're unsatisfied with the work:

  • We offer one round of revisions included in every project.
  • If we fail to deliver the agreed scope, we'll offer a partial or full refund.
  • Disputes should be raised within 7 days of delivery.

How to Request a Refund

  1. Email us at hello@sanzyai.com with your Gumroad order ID and reason for the refund.
  2. We'll review your request within 48 hours.
  3. If approved, the refund will be processed through Gumroad to your original payment method.

You can also request refunds directly through Gumroad: Gumroad Refund Help →

Questions?

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact us.
