## Internal linking rules per post
- Add one contextual link to /domain-finder when discussing domains, registrar pricing, renewals.
- Add one contextual link to /prompt-store when discussing prompts, image generation, productivity.
- Add one contextual link to /ai-services when discussing done-for-you setup or implementation.
- Use descriptive anchors, e.g. "compare live registrar prices", "browse premium prompt packs", "request AI service setup".

## Fast execution SOP (for 0-team or solo founder)
- Day before: choose tomorrow topic from table and collect top 5 SERP competitors.
- Draft: use one outline template (intro, comparison/steps, FAQs, CTA).
- On-page SEO: include keyword in title, H1, first 120 words, one H2, meta description.
- Add FAQ section with 3-5 real questions from People Also Ask.
- Publish and index: submit URL in Google Search Console.
- Refresh loop: after 14 days, update posts with low impressions (<100).

## KPIs to track weekly
- Indexed pages count
- Clicks + impressions per cluster
- Top 20 keyword movement
- CTR on money-page internal links
- Conversion events (lead form submits, pack clicks, domain-finder searches)
