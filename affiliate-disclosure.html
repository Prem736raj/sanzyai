Transparency about how we earn and our editorial independence.

Some links on SanzyAI.com — particularly in our Domain Price Finder and AI Tools Directory — are affiliate links. This means we may earn a small referral commission when you click through and make a purchase or sign up for a service.

This does not affect the price you pay. The cost to you is exactly the same whether you use our link or go directly to the provider's website.

Editorial Independence

Our recommendations, rankings, and comparisons are made independently. Affiliate partnerships do not influence:

  • Which tools we list or recommend.
  • The order in which results appear in our Domain Price Finder.
  • Our editorial opinions in reviews, guides, or news articles.

We only recommend tools and services we genuinely believe provide value. If a tool isn't good, we won't feature it — regardless of affiliate potential.

Why Affiliate Links?

Affiliate commissions are one of the ways we keep SanzyAI running and maintain free tools like the Domain Price Finder, AI Tools Directory, and Learning Hub. Your support through these links helps us continue building useful resources.

Any sponsored or paid content on SanzyAI will be clearly labeled as such. Sponsored listings in our tools directory are marked with a "Sponsored" badge so you always know the difference between editorial picks and paid placements.
