The Beginner's Guide: Zero to Hero

If you've never built a website before, start here. We break down the absolute basics.

1. What is a Website?

Think of it as your digital house. Your Domain is your address (e.g., mysite.com), and your Hosting is the land where your house sits.

2. Free vs Paid: The Reality

Free is great for learning and small projects. Paid is for professional businesses that need speed, reliability, and full control.
