Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

1. Introduction

SanzyAI ("we", "our", "us") operates the website sanzyai.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or purchase our products.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name and email address when you contact us or subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Payment Information: All payments are processed through Gumroad. We do not store your credit card details. Gumroad handles all payment data securely.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, browser type, device type, and referring URL — collected through standard analytics.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for site functionality (e.g., language preference, theme preference). We use Google Analytics for anonymous traffic data.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To deliver purchased products and provide customer support.
  • To send our newsletter (only if you subscribed — you can unsubscribe anytime).
  • To improve our website, tools, and content based on usage patterns.
  • To prevent fraud and abuse.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Gumroad — Payment processing and product delivery. Gumroad Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics — Anonymous website analytics.
  • Google Translate — Language translation widget.
  • Vercel / Cloudflare — Website hosting and CDN.

5. Data Retention

We retain your contact information only as long as needed to provide support and fulfill legal obligations. Newsletter subscribers can unsubscribe at any time, and we will delete their email upon request.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt out of our newsletter at any time.
  • Disable cookies through your browser settings.

7. Security

Our website uses HTTPS encryption. All payment processing is handled by Gumroad's PCI-compliant infrastructure. We do not store sensitive financial data on our servers.

8. Contact

For privacy-related requests, contact us at: hello@sanzyai.com
