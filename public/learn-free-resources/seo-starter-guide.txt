SEO Starter Guide

Step 1: Find one primary keyword per page.
Step 2: Place keyword in title, H1 and first paragraph.
Step 3: Keep URL short and descriptive.
Step 4: Add one internal link to another useful page.
Step 5: Add one external reference link where relevant.
Step 6: Use image alt text with context.
Step 7: Write a meta description under 160 chars.
Step 8: Publish consistently and update pages monthly.

Simple publishing rhythm:
- Week 1: 2 pages
- Week 2: 2 pages
- Week 3: refresh top page
- Week 4: add FAQ schema and improve CTA
