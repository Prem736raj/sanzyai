Domain Buying Checklist

1. Pick one brandable and short name.
2. Prefer .com when available.
3. Avoid hyphens and numbers.
4. Check trademark conflicts.
5. Enable domain privacy.
6. Set auto-renew to ON.
7. Point DNS to hosting provider.
8. Add CNAME for www.
9. Verify SSL after DNS propagation.
10. Save registrar and DNS login details.
