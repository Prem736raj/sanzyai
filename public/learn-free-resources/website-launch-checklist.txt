Website Launch Checklist

Technical
- Domain points correctly to host
- SSL certificate active (https)
- No broken links on core pages
- Mobile layout tested on 3 screen sizes
- Favicon, title and meta description set

Content
- Homepage headline is clear
- CTA above fold and in footer
- About and Contact pages published
- Privacy policy and terms published

Growth
- Google Search Console connected
- Analytics installed and events checked
- Sitemap submitted
- First 3 content pages published
- One lead magnet ready
