Best ChatGPT Prompts for Business in 2026

📅 April 2026 ✍️ SanzyAI Team 📂 Business / AI

If you're using ChatGPT for business but getting generic responses, the problem isn't the AI — it's your prompts. A well-structured prompt can turn ChatGPT into a strategist, copywriter, sales coach, and business analyst rolled into one.

Here are the best ChatGPT prompts for business, organized by category. Each one is copy-paste ready.

📋 Business Planning Prompts

1. One-Page Business Plan Generator

Act as a startup business consultant. Create a one-page business plan for a [BUSINESS TYPE] targeting [TARGET MARKET]. Include: value proposition, revenue model, key metrics, 90-day action plan, and estimated startup costs. Format as a structured document with clear sections.

2. Competitor Analysis Framework

Act as a competitive intelligence analyst. Analyze the competitive landscape for [YOUR INDUSTRY]. Identify the top 5 competitors, their strengths, weaknesses, pricing strategies, and market positioning. Provide actionable recommendations for differentiation.

3. SWOT Analysis

Create a detailed SWOT analysis for [YOUR BUSINESS/IDEA]. For each category (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats), provide at least 5 specific, actionable items. End with 3 strategic priorities based on the analysis.

📢 Marketing & Content Prompts

4. Content Calendar Generator

Act as a social media strategist. Create a 30-day content calendar for [BUSINESS TYPE] on [PLATFORM]. Include: post type (carousel, reel, story), caption hook, hashtag strategy, and best posting time. Focus on driving [GOAL: sales/engagement/followers].

5. Email Sequence Writer

Write a 5-email welcome sequence for [BUSINESS TYPE]. The goal is to nurture new subscribers from awareness to purchase. Include subject lines with 40%+ open rate potential, personalization tokens, and clear CTAs. Tone: [professional/casual/friendly].

6. SEO Blog Post Outline

Create a comprehensive SEO blog post outline for the keyword "[TARGET KEYWORD]". Include: H1 title (60 chars), meta description (155 chars), H2/H3 structure, key points for each section, internal linking opportunities, and a compelling conclusion with CTA.

💰 Sales & Customer Service

7. Sales Script Generator

Act as a sales trainer. Write a consultative sales script for selling [PRODUCT/SERVICE] to [TARGET CUSTOMER]. Include: opening hook, discovery questions, value presentation, objection handling for top 3 common objections, and closing technique.

8. Customer Service Response Templates

Create 10 customer service response templates for a [BUSINESS TYPE]. Cover: order inquiries, refund requests, product complaints, shipping delays, and positive feedback responses. Tone: empathetic but professional. Keep each under 100 words.

🔧 Operations & Productivity

9. Meeting Agenda Creator

Create a structured meeting agenda for a [MEETING TYPE] meeting lasting [DURATION]. Include: objectives, discussion topics with time allocations, decision items, and action items template. Ensure the agenda drives decisions, not just discussion.

10. Process Documentation

Act as an operations manager. Document the step-by-step process for [TASK/PROCESS] at [COMPANY TYPE]. Include: prerequisites, detailed steps, decision points, troubleshooting tips, and quality checkpoints. Format for easy onboarding of new team members.

Tips for Better Business Prompts

  1. Always assign a role — "Act as a [expert type]" dramatically improves output quality
  2. Be specific about format — Tell ChatGPT exactly how you want the output structured
  3. Include context — The more relevant details you provide, the better the response
  4. Chain prompts — Use the output of one prompt as input for the next
  5. Iterate — Ask ChatGPT to "improve this" or "make this more specific"
